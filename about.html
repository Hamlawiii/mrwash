Meet Mr. Wash

Founded in Mississauga in 2011, Mr. Wash has grown from a single mobile detailing van into a trusted GTA-wide team with one goal: deliver dealership-quality results with hometown service.

Why customers choose us
  • Locally owned & operated
  • IDA certified detailers & trainers
  • Eco-conscious water and waste systems
  • Transparent pricing & easy scheduling

Our Story

What started as weekend wash jobs for neighbours became a full-service detailing company trusted by GTA families, dealerships, and corporate fleets.

2011

Launched in Clarkson, Mississauga with one van and a mission to bring pro detailing directly to clients' driveways.

2015

Opened our first studio bay in Etobicoke to handle ceramic coatings and winter storage clients.

2019

Introduced electric-powered mobile units and water reclamation to reduce our environmental footprint.

2023

Expanded to four fully equipped vans, a training centre, and ongoing partnerships with GTA auto clubs.

Team Mr. Wash
