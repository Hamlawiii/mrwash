Meet Mr. Wash

Founded in Hamilton in 2025, Mr. Wash is a fresh, fast-growing detailing team built on dealership-quality standards and neighbourly service. From day one, we've focused on spotless interiors, mirror-finish exteriors, and a smooth, transparent experience across the GTA.

Why customers choose us
  • Locally owned & operated
  • Eco-conscious water and waste systems
  • Transparent pricing & easy scheduling

Team Mr. Wash

  • Detail-Oriented Experts: Every member of our team is trained to focus on the small things that make a big difference — from spotless interiors to mirror-finish exteriors.
  • Passionate About Cars: We're not just cleaners — we're car enthusiasts who treat every vehicle like our own.
  • Reliable & Professional: Our crew shows up on time, works efficiently, and ensures every customer drives away 100% satisfied.
  • Committed to Excellence: We take pride in delivering consistent, high-quality results that keep clients coming back.
